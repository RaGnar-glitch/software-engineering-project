PROGRAMS

UITS added the latest education system to its curriculum to developing the knowledge and skills of the students as well as integrated human values in the education system. UITS is a science and technological knowledge-based center that provides marketable skills for younger generations who may be gainfully employed both national and international organizations.

AFFORDABILITY

Merit based scholarships ‘Special Waiver’ can be extended to the students who seek admission in postgraduate programs (MBA/MSS/M.Sc. in Telecommunications Engineering/M.Sc. in CSE/LLM/MA in English, etc.) based on CGPA obtained in Bachelor/ Hons. /equivalent degrees.

CERTIFICATION

UITS forms a community of Bangladesh's brightest minds and most committed educators; and create opportunities for them to meet their academic and human potential. We believe in open and respectful interaction with students, and among its staff at all levels.

Welcome To UITS

University of Information Technology and Sciences (UITS), the first IT-based private university in Bangladesh was founded on 7 August 2003 as a non-profit organization. INFORMATION SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY SOLUTION LTD. (ISTS), a concern of PHP group headed by Alhaj Sufi Mohamed Mizanur Rahman Chowdhury is the sponsor of UITS.

As the first IT-based private university, UITS is playing an important role in the field of information technology and innovative technology. Considering the noble task in distributing knowledge, UITS have worked with the attitude of service to create skilled human resources.

Beautiful campus environment, study and research opportunities, co-curricular activities, industrial tour etc., have given new height to UITS. Intensive monitoring and friendly conduct to every student of UITS is playing a significant role in the development of student intellectual level. And for this reason, UITS is the first priority for all undergraduate students.
